## Geometry & Shape Translation

- **Pill-shaped**: Buttons, tags, chips — fully rounded ends (`rounded-full`).
- **Softly rounded**: Cards, containers — generous corner radius (16-20px).
- **Glassmorphism**: Semi-transparent surfaces with background blur, thin white/gray border, and soft shadow.
- **Circular**: Timer rings, avatar frames, floating action buttons.
- **Wave/Organic**: Background decorations, section dividers — flowing curves.

## Depth & Elevation (Dark Theme)

- **Flat**: No shadows, rely on surface color differences for depth.
- **Soft Glow**: Subtle colored shadow/glow beneath elevated elements (cyan or purple tint).
- **Floating**: Cards that appear to hover with soft diffused glow and slight scale.
- **Ambient**: Background elements with gentle opacity and blur for atmospheric depth.
